SpeechToText is an input method editor (IME) with on-device speech recognition. It supports the Whisper (TFLite), Parakeet (ONNX streaming), and Moonshine Base (English) engines—pick one in the app.

It can work as a standalone app and also offers an IME which can e.g. be activated
via the microphone button in <a href="https://f-droid.org/packages/helium314.keyboard/">HeliBoard</a>.

Whisper models are downloaded from Hugging Face on first use (large download). Parakeet and Moonshine use their own model downloads from the app. Internet is used for downloads and (depending on engine) not required after setup.

Voice recognition runs on your device (no cloud API in this build).

As a standalone app you can also translate supported languages to English with Whisper.

To get the most out of SpeechToText:
<ul>
<li>Press and hold the button while speaking (or use live transcription where supported)</li>
<li>Pause briefly before starting to speak</li>
<li>Speak clearly, loudly, and at a moderate pace</li>
<li>Hold-to-talk is limited to about 60 seconds per recording</li>
</ul>
